# Power BI Dashboard Description

## Dashboard Objective
The objective of this Power BI dashboard is to provide a consolidated and
interactive view of e-commerce sales performance, customer distribution,
product contribution, and regional trends. The dashboard is designed to help
stakeholders quickly understand overall business performance and identify
key revenue drivers.



## Key KPIs
The dashboard highlights the following key performance indicators at the top
for instant business overview:

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value (AOV)



## Visual Analysis

### Sales Trend by Year, Quarter, and Segment
A line chart displays total sales trends over time segmented by customer type
(Consumer, Corporate, Home Office). This visual helps identify growth patterns,
seasonality, and segment-wise performance changes across years.

### Category and Sub-Category Performance
A donut chart represents total sales contribution by Category and Sub-Category.
It helps in understanding which product segments contribute the most to overall
revenue and which segments underperform.

### Regional and Category Sales Distribution
A bar chart shows total sales across regions (Central, West, East, South)
segmented by product categories. This visual enables comparison of category
performance across different regions.

### Top Customers by Revenue
A horizontal bar chart highlights the top 10 customers based on total revenue.
This helps identify high-value customers and analyze revenue concentration.



## Business Value
- Provides a quick snapshot of overall sales performance
- Identifies top-performing product categories and regions
- Helps understand customer contribution and purchasing behavior
- Supports data-driven decisions related to inventory planning, marketing,
  and customer retention



## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- MySQL (SQL Analysis)
- Python (Pandas) for data Import.
- Excel (Data Preparation)
